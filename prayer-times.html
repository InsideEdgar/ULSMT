The Five Daily Prayers

الفجر

Transliteration: Fajr

Meaning: Dawn Prayer

Rak'ahs: 2 Sunnah, 2 Fard

الظهر

Transliteration: Dhuhr

Meaning: Noon Prayer

Rak'ahs: 4 Sunnah, 4 Fard, 2 Sunnah

العصر

Transliteration: Asr

Meaning: Afternoon Prayer

Rak'ahs: 4 Sunnah, 4 Fard

المغرب

Transliteration: Maghrib

Meaning: Sunset Prayer

Rak'ahs: 3 Fard, 2 Sunnah

العشاء

Transliteration: Isha

Meaning: Night Prayer

Rak'ahs: 4 Sunnah, 4 Fard, 2 Sunnah, Witr
